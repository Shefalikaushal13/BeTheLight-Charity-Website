ABOUT US

Welcome to Be The Light- Empowering Underprivileged Children for a Brighter Tomorrow

Our Mission and Vision

At Be The Light, we believe in "Breaking the cycle, before the cycle breaks you".
Be The Light is a Non Governmental Organisation and our core mission is to make a lasting impact on the lives of underprivileged children, empowering them to break free from the cycle of poverty and achieve their full potential.
We believe that every child deserves a fair chance to thrive, regardless of their socioeconomic background.
Through our unwavering commitment and tireless efforts, we aspire to create a world where no child is left behind, where dreams can be nurtured, and hope can flourish.

Who we are

Be The Light was established in 2004 with a heartfelt determination to address the urgent needs and challenges faced by underprivileged children in our community and beyond.
As a registered nonprofit organization, we are fueled by the passion of our dedicated team and the support of generous donors who believe in our cause.

Impact and Success Stories

Over the years, we have witnessed remarkable transformations in the lives of countless children we have supported. From improved academic performances to increased self-esteem, the impact of our programs is evident.
Many of our beneficiaries have gone on to pursue higher education and secure dignified careers, breaking the cycle of poverty for their families.
Meet Zara, a young girl from a low-income neighborhood who struggled to access education. With our intervention, Zara received educational support and mentorship, allowing her to excel academically.
Today, she is a confident college graduate with a dream of becoming a teacher to inspire and support children just like herself.

Our Programs and Initiatives

  1. Education for All: We firmly believe that education is the most potent weapon to combat poverty. Our "Education for All" program aims to provide quality education, essential school supplies, and scholarships to children from marginalized backgrounds. We collaborate with schools, educators, and volunteers to ensure that every child receives the educational opportunities they deserve.
  2. Health and Nutrition: Proper healthcare and nutrition are fundamental rights of every child. Our "Health and Nutrition" initiative focuses on providing medical check-ups, vaccinations, and nutritious meals to children in impoverished communities. We also conduct workshops on hygiene and sanitation to promote overall well-being.
  3. Empowerment through Inspiration: Inspirational stories of resilience and triumph can instill a sense of confidence and self-esteem in underprivileged children. When they realize that they, too, can overcome challenges and achieve greatness despite all the odds. They develop a more positive self-image, empowering them to face difficulties with courage and determination.
  4. Child Protection and Advocacy: Protecting children's rights is at the heart of our mission. We actively advocate for children's rights and work to eradicate child labor, exploitation, and abuse. Through community outreach and awareness campaigns, we strive to create a protective and supportive environment for all children.

How can I help?

  1. Donations: Your financial support can change lives. Even a small donation can make a significant difference in the life of an underprivileged child.
  2. Volunteering: Join our team of passionate volunteers and actively participate in our programs and events. Your time and dedication can have a lasting impact on a child's life.
  3. Sponsorship: Sponsor a child's education or well-being to provide them with the resources they need to succeed.
  4. Inspire: By getting in touch with a child over the phone or in person
  5. Corporate Partnerships: Partner with us as a corporate entity to support our cause and make a collective impact.
