About Us

Be The Light is a non governmental organisation that is dedicated to help underprivileged children by providing them with access to education, healthcare, and basic necessities.
Their mission is to empower children to break the cycle of poverty and reach their full potential.
In addition to our services, we also host fundraising events and community outreach programs to raise awareness and support for their cause.
They rely on the generosity of donors and volunteers to continue their important work, and are always looking for ways to expand their reach and impact.
